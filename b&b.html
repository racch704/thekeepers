Welcome to our B&B

Comfortable Accommodation in the Heart of The Cairngorms National Park

All of our rooms are provided with freshly laundered bedding and towels. Additionally, you will have a hairdryer, USB chargers and a door lock. For those rainy evenings, each room has a smart TV with full access to Netflix, Prime Video, and Disney+. The entire property is fully covered by fast, reliable wifi. During your stay, you can relax around the table in the large convivial kitchen, that is always kept warm by the AGA cooker. You will be welcome to use our fully equipped kitchen for preparing meals, or, if you prefer, you can bring food back from one of the local take-aways to eat here. We serve a continental breakfast in the kitchen from 7.30am. You will also be welcome to use our very comfortable sunroom to relax in, which has comfortable seating and panoramic windows looking out over the grounds. You are likely to see red squirrel and deer wandering the grounds, as well as a variety of birds, including pheasants and grouse. If you are exceptionally lucky, you may even catch sight of the elusive and highly endangered Scottish Wildcat, which only lives in this area.

Spey

Is the largest of the three rooms and has it's own private en-suite. It is a south-facing bright room with beautiful views of the garden, pond and countryside. Breakfast included. This room has a comfortable king-size bed, as well as TV and comfortable armchair with reading light. Please check below for full list of included amenities.

Truim

Comfortable double room with lots of natural light that looks out onto beautiful garden, pond, and countryside. Continental breakfast included.

Calder

Comfortable double room with beautiful garden and countryside views.
