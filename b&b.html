Welcome to our B&B


Comfortable Accommodation in the Heart of The Cairngorms National Park

You will have shared access to the kitchen, where you are welcome to cook and eat, as well as shared access to our beautiful sun-room, where you can relax in peace with great views of the grounds. The whole house benefits from superfast reliable broadband.
Our home is comfortable and unpretentious. You can relax around the table in the large convivial kitchen, that is always kept warm by the AGA cooker, or in our spectacular sun-room, which has comfortable seating and panoramic windows looking out over the grounds. The house has superfast reliable wifi throughout. We live here with our children (1 teenager and our young son - both very chilled)
You will be welcome to use the kitchen and will have a shelf in each of the fridge and freezer for your use. You will be expected to clean up after cooking and eating! You can also relax in the sunroom. You will not have access to the other bedrooms and we ask you not to enter any of them. Please note that the kitchen and dining facilities are shared with us and with other guests, as is the sun-room. There is ample parking and we also have bike racks if required.
