The Story of Glentruim Lodge
B&B and Pod


Comfortable Accommodation in the Heart of The Cairngorms National Park


Glentruim Lodge is a large country house nestled at the end of a private track in the heart of the Cairngorms National Park, just 20 minutes from Aviemore. Set amid stunning natural beauty, Glentruim Lodge enjoys spectacular views over the local hills and glens. During your visit, you are likely to encounter red squirrel and deer wandering the grounds, and you will see a variety of birds of prey, as well as the more numerous Pheasants and Grouse. If you are exceptionally lucky, you may even catch sight of the elusive and highly endangered Scottish Wildcat, which only lives in this area. Glentruim Lodge is just 200 metres from the river Spey where you can go fishing with a locally bought permit and is the perfect location to experience outdoor sporting activities such as skiing, sailing, hillwalking, climbing, mountain biking.

Breakfast

Continental Breakfast with Vegan Options
Room bookings include continental breakfast. This consists of freshly baked bread, fruit juice, a selection of cereals, yoghurts, tea and freshly ground coffee. If you’re vegan, please let us know and we’ll be happy to provide suitable alternatives
